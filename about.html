About Extraterrestrial Audio

Hi! I'm Evan,...

but you can call me ET. I'm a musician, sound designer, producer and audio engineer. My musical journey began at 12, when I got my first guitar. Since then I've had the pleasure of graduating from Full Sail University, experience touring with a band, and working with many artist live and in the studio.
